Classic Homemade Lasagna

Description

Indulge in layers of rich tomato sauce, perfectly seasoned ground beef, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella, all nestled between tender pasta sheets. This classic homemade lasagna recipe is a comforting and hearty dish that’s perfect for family dinners or special gatherings. Easy to make and full of flavor, it’s sure to become a favorite!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles – 12-15 sheets (cooked al dente)
  • Ground beef – 500g (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • Onion – 1 small, finely chopped
  • Garlic – 2-3 cloves, minced
  • Olive oil – 1-2 tablespoons
  • Tomato sauce – 2 cups
  • Tomato paste – 2 tablespoons
  • Crushed tomatoes – 1 can (about 400g)
  • Italian seasoning – 1 tablespoon (or a mix of oregano, basil, thyme)
  • Salt and pepper – to taste
  • Ricotta cheese – 1 ½ cups
  • Egg – 1 large
  • Mozzarella cheese – 3 cups shredded
  • Parmesan cheese – 1 cup grated
  • Fresh basil – optional, for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, sauté until softened.
  4. Add the ground beef (or meat mixture) and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  5. Stir in the tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, and 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese until well combined.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  8. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  9. Repeat the layers (sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of sauce and mozzarella on top.
  10. Sprinkle the remaining Parmesan cheese over the top.
  11. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25-30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  12. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
