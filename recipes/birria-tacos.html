Authentic Birria Tacos

Description

Birria tacos are a delicious blend of tender, slow-cooked beef or lamb, simmered in a rich, flavorful broth. These tacos are served with melted cheese inside crispy tortillas, dipped in the flavorful birria consomé for a truly mouthwatering experience. Perfect for a special dinner or a gathering, these tacos bring authentic Mexican street food right to your table!

Ingredients

  • Beef chuck roast – 1.5 kg (or lamb or goat)
  • Dried guajillo chilies – 4-5
  • Dried ancho chilies – 2
  • Tomatoes – 2 large
  • Onion – 1 large, quartered
  • Garlic – 4 cloves
  • Apple cider vinegar – 1/4 cup
  • Bay leaves – 2-3
  • Cloves – 2-3
  • Cumin – 1 tablespoon
  • Oregano – 1 tablespoon
  • Beef broth – 4 cups
  • Salt and pepper – to taste
  • Corn tortillas – 12-16
  • Mozzarella cheese – 2 cups shredded (or Mexican cheese blend)
  • Fresh cilantro – for garnish
  • Lime wedges – for serving

Steps

  1. Remove the stems and seeds from the guajillo and ancho chilies. Soak them in hot water for 10-15 minutes until softened.
  2. In a blender, combine the softened chilies, tomatoes, garlic, onion, apple cider vinegar, cumin, oregano, and cloves. Blend into a smooth paste.
  3. Season the beef with salt and pepper, then sear it in a large pot until browned on all sides. Remove from the pot and set aside.
  4. In the same pot, add the chili paste and sauté for 5 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Return the beef to the pot, add the bay leaves and beef broth, and bring everything to a boil.
  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 2.5-3 hours, or until the beef is tender and easily shreds with a fork.
  7. Remove the beef from the pot, shred it, and set aside. Strain the broth to remove solids, creating the birria consomé.
  8. Heat a small amount of oil in a skillet. Dip each tortilla into the birria consomé, then place it on the skillet.
  9. Top each tortilla with shredded cheese and birria meat. Fold the tortilla in half and cook until crispy, flipping once.
  10. Serve the tacos hot with cilantro and lime wedges, and dip them into the remaining consomé for extra flavor.
