Quick and Easy Fried Rice

Description

This fried rice recipe is a delicious, flavorful dish packed with vegetables, tender scrambled eggs, and savory soy sauce. It's the perfect way to use leftover rice, and it can be customized with your favorite protein like chicken, shrimp, or tofu. Whip it up in just 20 minutes for a quick weeknight dinner or a satisfying side dish!

Ingredients

  • Cooked white rice – 3 cups (preferably cold)
  • Vegetable oil – 2 tablespoons
  • Onion – 1 small, diced
  • Garlic – 2 cloves, minced
  • Frozen peas and carrots – 1 cup
  • Eggs – 2 large, lightly beaten
  • Soy sauce – 3 tablespoons
  • Sesame oil – 1 teaspoon
  • Green onions – 2, chopped
  • Salt and pepper – to taste
  • Optional: chicken, shrimp, or tofu – 1 cup, cooked and chopped

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced onion and garlic, sauté for 2-3 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in the frozen peas and carrots, cooking for another 3-4 minutes until tender.
  4. Push the vegetables to the side of the pan, then add the eggs to the other side. Scramble them until fully cooked.
  5. Add the cold rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps with a spatula.
  6. Pour in the soy sauce and sesame oil, stirring everything together until well combined and heated through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. If using a protein like chicken, shrimp, or tofu, add it at this point and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  9. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.
